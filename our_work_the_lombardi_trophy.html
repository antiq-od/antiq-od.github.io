The Lombardi Trophy

Home - Our Work - The Lombardi Trophy

Green Bay Packers

In 2014, the Green Bay Packers commissioned Foam by Design to design and build a replica of the NFL's most revered trophy, the Lombardi Trophy monument. It was created in seven major pieces and three smaller pieces with a metal and foam infrastructure. The pieces were then shipped to and assembled at Lambeau Stadium, the home of the Green Bay Packers .The individual pieces were finished with chrome over a 10 week period by a team led by Dan Gokey of Insane Paint. The end result, a massive, 50′ tall, 14.5 ton icon that sits in the new multi-story atrium.
